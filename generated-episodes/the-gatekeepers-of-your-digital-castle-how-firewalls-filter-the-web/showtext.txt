The Gatekeepers of Your Digital Castle: How Firewalls Filter the Web
In this episode, Herman Poppleberry delves into the fascinating world of firewalls, explaining how these digital guardians manage to block unwanted external traffic while still allowing you to browse the internet, stream videos, and connect with the world. He clarifies the nuances of WAN to LAN traffic, stateful packet inspection, and the essential role firewalls play in maintaining network security without hindering legitimate online activity. Herman might also ponder the existential challenges of a donkey trying to use a web browser.
